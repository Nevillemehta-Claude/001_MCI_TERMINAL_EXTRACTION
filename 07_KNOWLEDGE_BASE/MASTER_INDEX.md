## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: What is MCI?
**A:** Mission Control Interface - a decision-support trading dashboard for Indian markets. It's the COCKPIT, not the ENGINE. It monitors but does NOT trade.

### Q: What is CIA-SIE-PURE?
**A:** The trading ENGINE. It executes actual trades. MCI sends START/STOP commands to it.

### Q: When do tokens expire?
**A:** 6:00 AM IST daily (CR-004 SACRED). This equals 00:30 UTC.

### Q: What's the shutdown sequence?
**A:** 6 steps per CR-002: Confirm → Notify → Wait → Close → Log → Complete

### Q: What are the 7 UXMI states?
**A:** idle, hover, active, loading, success, error, disabled

### Q: What timing constants are used?
**A:** hover=150ms, active=100ms, tooltip=300ms, toast=5000ms
